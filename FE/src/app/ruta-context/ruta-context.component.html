CONTEXT

What is?

Páramo is a high altitude region that supply services to densely populated cities, vulnerable to global change (Buytaert et al., 2011).

Intense centennial degradation mainly grazing, burning and agriculture (Molina et al., 2015).

Soils primary control of flows – undisturbed (Correa et al., 2016; 2017).

Few on disturbed areas (Ochoa-Tocachi et al., 2016).

Scientific gaps:

* Understanding of water and solute fluxes in soils (Minaya et al., 2016; Hamel et al., 2017).

* The hydrological function of soils within a changing context + profile/hillslope/watershed approach (Hamel et al ., 2017).
